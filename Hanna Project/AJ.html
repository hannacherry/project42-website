About AJ!

This is a organization we have created to honor the life of A.J. Flatt, who left this Earth in 2021 at just 16 years old. A.J. was a sophomore at Chester County High School, who was a member of the Marching Eagles, Hosa, and drama club, and also loved being outside. AJ was an avid outdoorsman and sports fanatic. Project 42 exists to keep the memory of our son alive. We aim to join with our community in areas of outreach that helps meet the needs of others.
